ARTICLE 12

SOURCE OF INCOME/AUDIT

1. Membership: membership fee shall be either one off payment of £100 for the life member or £20 for ordinary membership. Ordinary membership shall be for one term.

2. Voluntary donation.

3. Organising events and other social functions/activities.

4. The Register Auditor shall certify the final audit of the Samaj before Annual General Meetings.





ARTICLE 13

IMPEACHMENT

Any members shall be impeached by two third majority decision of the Executive Committee. The members can be impeached on the following conditions:

1. Misuse of funds.

2. Immoral and undignified acts which tends to defame the Samaj.

3. If an executive member absent three times in the regular meeting, would be terminated without any notice.

Note: However, the member shall be given the opportunity to produce clarification before the impeachment.





ARTICLE 14

AMENDMENT OF CONSTITUTION

Amendments to any article, clause or any particular word mentioned in the existing constitution, must be submitted in writing to the Executive Committee. The proposed amendment shall be circulated to all members for their winder views and opinions. Once the amendment is accepted by the Committee then it shall be presented at the AGM for the majority consent. If the bill is endorsed by the AGM then the clauses shall be amended.
