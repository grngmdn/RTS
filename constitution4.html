ARTICLE 9

RIGHTS AND DUTIES OF THE COMMITTEE MEMBERS

1. Chairman

• Shall preside the meetings;

• Shall represent on behalf of Samaj;

• Shall call emergency meetings in case of necessary;

• Shall accept the resignation tendered by the Committee Member;

• Shall cast the vote in the event of equal tie;



2. Vice-Chairman

• Shall preside the meeting in the absence of chairman;

• Shall be responsible for any other tasks as assigned by the Executive Committee;



3. General secretary

• Shall be responsible for all formal and informal communication;

• Shall be responsible for calling meetings consulting with the Chairman;

• Shall prepare agendas of all meetings consulting with the Chairman;

• Shall maintain minutes of the meetings;

• Shall table all the formal and informal documents including annual report in Annual General Meeting;



4. Secretary

• Shall maintain all the duties in the absence of General Secretary;

• Shall furnish any other additional tasks as assigned by the Executive Committee;



5. Treasurer

• Shall be responsible for all the financial transactions;

• Shall be responsible for collection of membership subscription and other income;

• Shall table the Financial Annual Report in the meeting and get the report certified by the Registered Chartered Accountant before the Annual General Meetings;

• Shall open and manage a joint Bank Account in the name of ‘Reading Tamu Samaj’. The Bank Account would be operated by the joint signatures of any three Executive Committee Members including the Chairman, Treasurer and one person as designated by the Executive Committee Meeting;



6. Assistant treasurer

• Shall be responsible for all the financial transactions in the absence of Treasurer;

• Shall perform any other tasks as assigned by the Executive Committee;



7. The Co-Ordinators

• The housing: The Housing Co-ordinator shall be responsible for RTS. He/She will be responsible to initiate a plan for fund raising and set up a Housing Committee;

• The Cultural: The cultural Co-ordinator shall be responsible in preserving and promoting Gurungs culture, tradition and way of life and extends it wider value to the younger generations;

• The Youths and Sports: The youths and Sports Co-ordinator shall guide and encourage to our younger generation in the various sports and other social activities;

• The Mothers Group: This group shall be responsible in providing welfare and social support to RTS;

• Seminar and Events: The seminar and Events Co-ordinator shall be responsible in conducting Seminars and Events by inviting scholars or any other expertise which might be beneficial to RTS;
