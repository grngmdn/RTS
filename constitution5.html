ARTICLE 10

MEETING

1. Calendar Events. As soon as the new Executive Committee resumes the office, the committee shall publish the Annual Calendar Events.

2. The Committee Meetings shall be held in last Saturday in every two months period.

3. The Chairman shall call an emergency and special meeting in the event of urgency.





ARTICLE 11

ELECTION/QUALIFICATION

1. The Executive Committee shall nominate three member’s Election Commission to conduct the election of the Executive Committee. The Commission may formulate rules and regulations in order to conduct the election.

2. The tenure of the committee shall be for a period of 2 years. 3. RTS shall follow any financial returns as per the British Fiscal Year.

4. The term of Executive Committee shall end in December month. Annual General Meeting and the election of the Executive Committee shall be held in the same month.

5. Casual vacancies arising due to resignation or any other reason shall be filled by the Executive Committee for the remaining term.

6. The Executive Committee shall elect the new Executive Committee on the basis of the past performances of the individual. The direct voting shall be discouraged wherever possible.



7. The qualification to contest the election shall be on the following conditions

• The candidate for the President must be a life or the ordinary member;

• The candidate for the President must have served at least one term for any post in the Executive Committee.

• The remaining positions of the Committee, if vacant, due to resignation or any other reasons, shall be selected by the Executive Committee for the remaining term.



THE ELIGIBILITY FOR THE VOTING RIGHTS

1. Aged 18 or over.

2. Ordinary and the life member shall have the right to cast the vote.

3. Every effort shall be made to fill the posts without having conducting the election. Voting shall be the last resort.
