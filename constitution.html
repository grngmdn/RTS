READING TAMU SAMAJ CONSTITUTION

(ESTABLISHED IN 2003)
ADOPTED ON 22ND SEPTEMBER 2012

PREAMBLE


ARTICLE 1

BACKGROUND INTRODUCTION

Reading Tamu Samaj was formerly known as Reading Tamu Dhee, initially established in 2003. In view of the growing number of Gurungs in Reading area and to accommodate them all into one umbrella, the Annual General Meeting held on 22 September 2012 unanimously decided to amend its name from “Reading Tamudhee” to “Reading Tamu Samaj”.





ARTICLE 2

NAME

The name: The official name of the association shall be Reading Tamu Samaj”. The name shall be used as a short form RTS.





ARTICLE 3

RECOGNITION

Reading Tamu Samaj (RTS) stands for all Gurungs from various parts of Nepal living in Reading and its surrounding area. RTS shall preserve Gurungs identity and promote its culture and tradition values.





ARTICLE 4

THE OBJECTIVES

The objectives of the Reading Tamu Samaj shall be as follows:

1. To preserve and promote Gurung culture and tradition;

2. Social cohesion and integration within Gurungs as one family;

3. To manage all kind of possible support in time of need and financial hardship;

4. Shall maintain and foster relations by organising various social and festivity events for the co-operation and benefits of the community as a whole;

5. To establish and promote good relationship and co-operation with other communities and Borough Councils;

6. To organise all kinds of events as endorsed by the Executive Committee;

7. Endeavour to purchase own property;
