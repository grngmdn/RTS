ARTICLE 5

MEMBERSHIP

The membership shall be provided only to those Gurungs who are resided in Reading Borough Council and its surrounding area. However, our ‘chelibeti’ (sisters and daughters) who are married and residing outside Reading may also obtain the membership if they wish, and shall be provided with the same level of treatment and benefits as our members. Type of membership:

1. Life Member: This is a permanent membership. The life membership fee shall be £100 per family. It covers the unmarried children under the age of 21, provided resided in the same property.

2. General member: This is a renewal membership. General Membership fee shall be of £20 per term. Each term will be of two years period. It covers married dependants and under the age of 21.

3. Honorary member: The Executive Committee may grant Honorary Membership to those individuals who have contributed notable assistance to the RTS. But such Honorary member shall not have a voting right of the Samaj.





ARTICLE 6

SOCIAL AND WELFARE SUPPORT

1. Death. In case of death, £1500 shall be provided to the next to kin of the Life Member and £500 for the General Member. Furthermore, all the members under this constitution shall contribute minimum of £10. The contribution is called ‘Syora’. The committee shall manage and co-operate to organize the funeral services.

2. Serious Illness: A get well card and moral support will be arranged by the Committee and members.

3. Destitution: Possible financial support through voluntary contribution/donation. Help the family in destitution to apply for state benefits that they are entitled.

4. Marriage/Death: A card + flower shall be arranged.
